Lab for the Recently Possible

About

Lab for the Recently Possible (L4RP) is a space to learn, experiment, collaborate and incubate. Blending the edge of tech with social innovation, art and culture. If there's something you're working on that you don't quite know how to achieve, perhaps the Lab can help out.

We're an initiative set up by Dharmafly, a local web apps design and development company. We're in a collaborative workspace building called "The Dock", a sister project of The Werks.

We are here

We're in Brighton & Hove, on the south coast of England - a few minutes walk from the beautiful seaside, right by the Sussex County Cricket Ground, and just an hour away from London.

Our entrance is on Wilbury Villas, Hove, BN3 3EL. (See below for directions)

Get here

Trains

Hove station
7 minutes walk away (walking directions). Trains to London Victoria leave regularly and take 1 hour.
Brighton station
25 minutes walk away (directions), with trains all over. Bus #7 from the station goes straight to Wilbury Villas.

Local buses

The closest buses stop nearby on Cromwell Road, at the bottom of Wilbury Villas. Bus #7 travels between both train stations and the city centre. Bus #81(A/B/C/E) goes from London Road to Brighton centre to Hove.

Many more buses stop at Palmeira Square, a few minutes walk away: 1, 1A, 2, 2A, 5, 5A, 5B, 6, 20X, 25, 26, 46, 49, 49A, 71.
