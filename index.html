Lab for the
Recently Possible

A space to learn, experiment, collaborate and incubate projects at the edges of technology, art, culture and society. If you're exploring something that you haven't yet mastered, perhaps the Lab for the Recently Possible can help?

We've moved! We're now on Gloucester Street, in the North Laine of Brighton, on the south coast of England.

L4RP is a social project by Dharmafly, a Brighton-based tech company who design and build web apps.

The one rule is, if someone needs help and you know how to help them, then you have to help them.
