Lab for the Recently Possible

A space to learn, experiment, collaborate and incubate projects at the edges of technology, social movements, art and culture. If you're exploring something that you haven't yet mastered, perhaps the Lab for the Recently Possible can help?

L4RP is a social project by Dharmafly, a Brighton-based company who design and develop web apps. We're based at a new collaborative workspace called "The Dock" (a sister project of The Werks), which provides offices and freelancer coworking space for the collaborative, creative industries.
