Lab for the Recently Possible

A space to learn, experiment, collaborate and incubate projects at the edges of technology, social movements, art and culture. If you're exploring something that you don't yet understand, perhaps the Lab for the Recently Possible can help?

L4RP is a social project by Dharmafly, a Brighton-based company who create web apps for socal impact. We're based at a collaborative workspace called "The Dock", a sister project of The Werks (with plenty of new offices and freelancer coworking space for the creative industries).
